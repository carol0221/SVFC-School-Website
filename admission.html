Admission Process

Step 1

Application Form

Fill out the Student Application Form (can be found at the bottom of this page). Click submit once accomplished. A notification will be sent to your email.

Note: Telephone inquiries will not be entertained.

>

Step 2

Upload Documents

Scan and upload your document via Google Form link to your email. A confirmation will follow to proceed to Step 3.

Step 3

Admission Pay

Pay ₱800 via DragonPay (link sent in the confirmation email). Proof of payment will be emailed to you.

Step 4

Schedule Test

Choose your test schedule via Google Appointment Form. Bring your test permit for onsite or await the test link for online.

Note: Telephone inquiries will not be entertained.

Step 5

Application Status

You will receive one of the following:
✔ Accepted: Student Acceptance Contract (SAC)
✔ Conditional: Interview + Conditional Acceptance Contract (CAC)
✖ Not Accepted: End of admissions Submit signed documents online.

>

Step 6

Pre-Enrollment

Sign the Pre-Enrollment Agreement Form (PEAF). Pay the reservation fee (online via DragonPay or onsite). Get your endorsement slip and prepare for final enrollment via the Registrar's Portal.
